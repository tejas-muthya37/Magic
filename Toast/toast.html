Toasts

Toasts can be used to show alerts to the user.

Success Toast

Success Toast can be used to inform the user about a positive outcome of an event.

We have received your order! 
              
<div class="toast toast-success">
    We have received your order!
</div>

Danger Toast

Danger Toast can be used to inform the user about a negative outcome of an event.

Your request was not processed! 
              
<div class="toast toast-danger">
    Your request was not processed!
</div>
