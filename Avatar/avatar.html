Avatars

Avatars are generally used on Messaging, Social Media and Video Calling Apps. Use the .avatar class to add Avatars to your website.

Profile Avatars

A default Profile Avatar is of the size 50x50 pixels.

              
    <img class="avatar" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-1" />
    <img class="avatar" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-2" />
    <img class="avatar" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-3" />

Profile Avatars of different sizes

Profile Avatars come in 3 sizes. Use the sm | md | lg for small, medium and large Avatars respectively. 

              
    <img class="avatar sm" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-1" />
    <img class="avatar md" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-2" />
    <img class="avatar lg" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-3" />

Square Avatars

Square Avatars are default avatars with slightly curved borders. 

              
    <img class="avatar sm square" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-1" />
    <img class="avatar md square" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-2" />
    <img class="avatar lg square" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="avatar-3" />
