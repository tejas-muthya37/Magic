Images

Images can add great value to the aesthetics of a website. Use the .image class to add our images to your website.

Simple Image

A simple image of 150x150 pixels.

              
<img class="image" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="img-1" />

Circular Image

Circular images are often used as profile pictures.

              
<img class="image circle" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="img-2" />

Resizable Image

Resizable images automatically change their height and width according to the screen size. 

              
<img class="image resizable" src="<your-image-url-here>" alt="img-3" />
