Cards

Cards add a dash of visual flavour to your webpages! Use the .card class to add our cards to your website.

Simple Text Card

These cards can be used to convey simple information through text.

Our Changing Planet

by Kurt Wagner

Visit ten places on our planet that are undergoing the biggest changes today. Some of them are almost unrecognizable to an old eye. Change is inevitable, so let's bring about a good one. 

              
<div class="card">
<div class="text">
    <h3>Our Changing Planet</h3>
    <h4>by Kurt Wagner</h4>
</div>
<p>Visit ten places on our planet that are
    undergoing the biggest changes today. Some
    of them are almost unrecognizable to an old eye.
    Change is inevitable, so let's bring about a good one.
</p>
</div>
</div>

Dismissable Cards

These cards can be dismissed and reloaded on the click of a button.

Our Changing Planet

by Kurt Wagner

-

Visit ten places on our planet that are undergoing the biggest changes today.

READ BOOKMARK
+
              
<script>
  const dismiss = document.querySelector(".dismiss");
  const reload = document.querySelector(".reload");
  const cardToDismiss = document.querySelector(".dismiss-card");
            
  dismiss.addEventListener("click", function () {
    cardToDismiss.classList.add("dismissed");
    reload.classList.add("reloaded");
  });
            
  reload.addEventListener("click", function () {
    cardToDismiss.classList.remove("dismissed");
    reload.classList.remove("reloaded");
  });
</script>

<div class="card dismiss-card card-shadow">
  <div class="dismiss-icon-with-text">
    <div class="text text-top">
      <h3>Our Changing Planet</h3>
      <h4>by Kurt Wagner</h4>
    </div>
    <div class="dismiss">-</div>
</div>

  <img
    src="<your-image-url-here>"
    alt=""
  />
  <p>
    Visit ten places on our planet that are
    undergoing the biggest changes today.
  </p>
