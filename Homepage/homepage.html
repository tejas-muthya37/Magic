Introduction

Magic is a CSS Component Library designed to help beginners focus on functionality and not worry about design. We know how tough it is to get good at CSS. It's normal to sweat it in the beginning. While you are learning the fundamentals of the web, feel free to use our components to make your site look good.

The styling has been done for you. All you have to do it is add a link to our CSS file, copy the mark-up and be on your way to create magic! Let's take a look at how to do that below.

Guide

Copy the below line of code into the <head> tag of your HTML. 

            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://magic-library.netlify.app/master.css">
